Residential Services

Trust T. Basch Inc. for careful home excavations

Since 1938, T. Basch Inc. has been leading the way for excavation projects in Northern Michigan, including Grand Traverse and Leelanau Counties. You won't find another company that has our combination of experience and dedication with the personal touch that bring peace of mind.

Residential excavation is a challenge because it isn't just any home and property - it's your home and property. It is your family who might need to temporarily vacate your home while someone digs in or around it. You can't trust it to just anyone, but you can trust it to T. Basch Inc.

Working throughout Michigan for over 75 years

Whether it's a large excavation such as developments and apartment complexes, or smaller projects like subdivisions and apartments, you can be sure that T. Basch Inc. has handled them all with accuracy and excellence. The only choice left is when you want us to get started.

With over 75 years of experience, you can rest assured that your project is in good hands. Our customers are our first priority, so you can expect top quality work. We also offer a one-year warranty on most services.
